Product Overview
SECTION ONE
A drawpit, sized to suit

Modular rings.
Site-built depth.

RHINO drawpits are rectangular access chambers built from interlocking rings of recycled polymer. Modular construction means depth is set on site - just stack rings until you hit grade. Length and width are picked from a standard range or made bespoke.

Six load ratings from A15 (pedestrian only) to F900 (airport apron) cover every typical use case for utility, telecoms and ducted networks. The polymer body is lightweight, chemical resistant, and a fraction of the cost of an equivalent concrete chamber.

Lighter than concrete, faster to install
A polymer drawpit weighs a fraction of its concrete equivalent, drops into the trench by hand, and gives the same load capacity. Install times measured in minutes per ring, not hours per chamber.
