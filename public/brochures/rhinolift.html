SECTION ONE
Reliable Water Movement

Move water, up.
Keep sites running.

RHINOLIFT Packaged Pumping Stations are designed to provide reliable, efficient and robust solutions for managing foul water, stormwater and grey water. From single-dwelling residential to large-scale commercial applications, RHINOLIFT systems flex to specific project needs with a range of single and dual-pump configurations.

Constructed from MDPE and GRP chambers, RHINOLIFT pumping stations provide exceptional strength and durability. Each station is equipped with vortex or macerator pumps to handle varying flow rates and ensure efficient, trouble-free operation. Stations are built and delivered to site within 24 to 48 hours, allowing you to meet even the tightest deadlines.

How It Works
SECTION TWO
Gravity Is Optional

Pump low-lying flow
to higher ground.

RHINOLIFT pumping stations are designed to move foul water, stormwater or grey water from low-lying areas to higher elevations for onward treatment or disposal. Vortex or macerator pumps handle solids of up to 50mm diameter. Dual-pump configurations provide duty-standby resilience, with one pump running while the other stands ready.

The packaged approach eliminates the need for gravity drainage where site conditions make it impractical or impossible. From basement developments to commercial hard-standing, RHINOLIFT provides the hydraulic lift without the complexity of bespoke build-on-site pump chambers.
