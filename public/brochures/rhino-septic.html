Product Overview
SECTION ONE
Off-mains wastewater treatment

Built for sites the
sewer never reached.

Where there’s no mains sewer, a septic tank is the standard route. RHINO septic tanks are one-piece rotomoulded HDPE - leak-proof by design, light enough for a small digger, and shaped to drop into variable ground without specialist plant.

Sized to population equivalent and daily flow, with primary or secondary treatment options. Discharge to watercourse, soakaway or drainfield - chosen against the site’s ground conditions and Environment Agency General Binding Rules.
