SECTION ONE
Stormwater Treatment, Engineered

Vortex separation
for cleaner runoff.

The SuDS RHINO Hydrodynamic Separator is designed for advanced stormwater management, specifically engineered to remove suspended solids, silt, hydrocarbons and debris from runoff. Patented vortex technology mitigates contaminants before they enter downstream infrastructure.

Achieving Mitigation Indices of 5-4-5, the system delivers industry-leading filtration and sediment capture while maximising the efficiency of stormwater treatment. Available in four diameter options, the SEHDS flexes to a wide range of site sizes and catchment conditions.

How It Works
SECTION TWO
Vortex Separation Technology

Swirl, settle,
release clean.

Vortex technology directs stormwater into a controlled spiral. Larger particles, sediment and contaminants are forced to the outer wall and settle out of the flow, while cleaner water continues through the system. No moving parts, no external power.

The SEHDS is manufactured from Glass-Reinforced Plastic using the latest composite technology for a lightweight yet highly durable vessel. The one-piece construction eliminates joints and leak points, ensuring long-term performance even under harsh environmental conditions.
