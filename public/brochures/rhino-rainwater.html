Product Overview
SECTION ONE
Two wins from the same install

Cuts mains demand,
cuts drainage loading.

A RHINO rainwater harvesting tank captures roof runoff, filters it, and feeds it back into the building for non-potable uses - WC flushing, irrigation, washdown, laundry. The result: lower mains water bills and a smaller stormwater discharge to the sewer.

Five tank sizes from 1000 L (small house) to 3300 L (commercial) cover most projects. Three system types let you match the building’s plumbing and pressure requirements, with a mains backup so the system never runs dry in extended dry spells.

Direct, Indirect or Gravity
Direct supply runs reused water straight to the appliance. Indirect tops up a header tank for pressure stability. Gravity feeds purely from a high-level reservoir. We’ll match the system to your building.
