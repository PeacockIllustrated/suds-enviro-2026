Product Overview
SECTION ONE
Polishing where it matters

A pollutant filter
you drop straight in.

RHINO POD is a patented floating filter that sits inside an existing chamber, manhole or catch basin and pulls hydrocarbons, heavy metals and organic pollutants out of stormwater as it passes. No civils, no power, no plumbing - it floats on the water surface and gets to work.

Available standalone for retrofitting to existing infrastructure, or factory-fitted to a RHINO SEHDS hydrodynamic separator for a single supply-and-fit polishing solution. A visible saturation indicator shows when the filter media needs swapping.
