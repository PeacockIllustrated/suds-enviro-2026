Product Overview
SECTION ONE
Engineered For Stormwater Control

Restrict discharge.
Protect downstream.

The SuDS RHINO SERF Series is specifically engineered for the efficient management of stormwater. A strategically sized orifice restricts the passage of water to a pre-set maximum rate, maintaining optimal water levels and preventing excessive runoff during storm events.

Chambers are delivered as factory-fitted one-piece units with an integral service drain-down capability that can be activated from ground level. This makes SERF chambers a highly efficient, user-friendly, plug-and-play stormwater management solution.

How It Works
SECTION TWO
Orifice Flow Regulation

A calibrated aperture,
calmly holding peak.

An orifice plate sized to the design head of the catchment controls the volume of water released from the chamber. During storm events, the chamber holds back peak flow, releasing at a controlled rate and protecting downstream infrastructure from overload.

The SERF Series is manufactured from High-Density Polyethylene using advanced extrusion and thermoforming technologies. Chambers arrive fully assembled as a one-piece unit, designed to handle both light-duty and full-traffic loading. The thermoformed base enhances structural strength and prevents fractures, ensuring a reliable installation.
