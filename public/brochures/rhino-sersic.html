Engineered For Surface Water

The RHINO SERSIC Series are prefabricated, factory-built HDPE chambers engineered for efficient stormwater and drainage management. Each chamber features a main channelled base with optional side connections, seamlessly integrating with the wider RHINO adaptor range.

Constructed from High-Density Polyethylene using advanced extrusion and thermoforming technologies, every chamber arrives fully assembled. Suitable for both light-duty and full-traffic loading applications, the range flexes to numerous project requirements without sacrificing structural integrity or hydraulic performance.

How It Works
SECTION TWO
Benched Channel Design

Sloped benches guide
flow, not blockages.

The benched base has sloped surfaces that guide water into the central flow channel, ensuring debris and sediment are directed into the main drainage path rather than settling in corners. The result is smooth, uninterrupted flow and far less maintenance over the chamber's service life.

The central channel follows the profile of the connected drainage pipes, minimising turbulence and enhancing hydraulic efficiency. Stormwater can flow freely through the chamber, even under heavy load, without the risk of sediment buildup. The benched design also makes inspection and cleaning straightforward for maintenance crews.
