Engineered For Foul Water

Constructed from High-Density Polyethylene using advanced extrusion and thermoforming technologies, the chambers arrive fully assembled. Suitable for light-duty and full-traffic loading, the range meets the demands of residential, commercial and industrial foul water systems without compromise.

How It Works
SECTION TWO
Benched Channel Design

Sloped surfaces stop
solids from settling.

The benched base uses sloped surfaces to guide foul water into the central flow channel, directing solids and sediments into the main drainage path rather than letting them settle in corners. Blockages are reduced and flow remains smooth through the life of the system.

The central channel follows the profile of the connected drainage pipes, minimising turbulence and enhancing hydraulic efficiency. Foul water flows freely even under heavy load. The benched form factor also makes inspection, rodding and cleaning straightforward for maintenance teams.
