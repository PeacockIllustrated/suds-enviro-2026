Product Overview
SECTION ONE
Engineered For SuDS Performance

Six diameters,
two stages of filtration.

The SuDS RHINO Advanced Plus 600 Series is specifically engineered for the efficient catchment and removal of silt and debris from impermeable or hard-surface areas. Paths, driveways, patios, roofs, car parks, roads and highways all benefit from a primary and secondary filtration system that captures contaminants before they enter downstream drainage.

When integrated into a Sustainable Drainage System (SuDS), the SERDS delivers enhanced performance and environmental protection, supporting compliance with regulatory requirements for flood mitigation and water treatment. Six diameter options cover everything from small residential lots to major civil schemes.

How It Works
SECTION TWO
Primary And Secondary Capture

A dual system
for cleaner flow.

The Advanced Plus Catch-Pit Chambers use a dual filtration system to separate and remove silt, debris and other contaminants from surface water runoff. Primary filtration catches larger particles, while secondary filtration captures finer sediments. Stormwater flows into the chamber, solids settle out, and filtered water is discharged into the drainage network.

The result is significantly reduced load on downstream infrastructure, improved water quality and a much lower risk of blockages in pipes and manholes. All of it in a factory-assembled HDPE one-piece chamber that is in the ground within a fraction of the time of a traditional in-situ build.
