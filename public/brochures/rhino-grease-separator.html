Product Overview
SECTION ONE
Big-volume FOG separation

For kitchens that
can’t fit it under the sink.

Where a Rhino GT under-sink unit isn’t enough, the Rhino Grease Separator handles serious volume below ground. Multi-chamber design gives more retention time, more storage capacity, and longer intervals between empties for high-output sites.

Sized to peak covers per day and design flow rate, each unit includes an integral sample point so trade-effluent monitoring takes minutes, not hours. HDPE construction is leak-proof and resistant to caustic kitchen-cleaning chemistry.
