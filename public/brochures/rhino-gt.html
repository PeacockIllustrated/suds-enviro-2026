Product Overview
SECTION ONE
FOG management, sized to suit

Four sizes,
one tough HDPE shell.

The Rhino GT range protects sewers and downstream treatment by capturing fats, oils and grease at source. Four model sizes scale from a small cafe to a high-volume hotel kitchen, all in one-piece rotomoulded HDPE that resists hot wash water and aggressive cleaning chemicals.

Each unit uses an integral baffle system to slow incoming wastewater, allowing FOG to rise and settle while clean water passes through to the outlet. Fits under-sink for low-output sites or floor-mounted for higher-volume kitchens.
